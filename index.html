Artifact 01
Stock Market Research Assistant
AI Chatbot · Conversational Agent
Objective
Design and build an AI-powered conversational chatbot that helps retail investors and students research stocks, analyze market trends, compare investment options, and understand financial data — all through natural language, without requiring expertise in complex financial platforms.
Process
Applied the full Design Thinking process (Empathy → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test). Identified user pain points through empathy mapping — financial data scattered across sites, jargon-heavy content, no affordable research tool. Selected a conversational AI assistant as the highest-feasibility solution after evaluating four alternatives. Prototyped using ChatGPT's CustomGPT builder with a structured 10-question planning framework covering scope, tone, guardrails, and test scenarios. Conducted testing across 7 scenarios: single stock analysis, stock comparisons, sector research, financial term definitions, out-of-scope handling, follow-up context retention, and unknown ticker handling. Iterated based on results — adding mandatory risk disclosures, enforcing disclaimer rules on every analysis response, and tuning temperature settings for consistency.
Tools
ChatGPT (GPT-4o) CustomGPT Builder NLP / LLM Design Thinking Prompt Engineering
